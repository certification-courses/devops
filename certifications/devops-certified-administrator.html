DevOps Certified Administrator

The DevOps Administrator is responsible for installation, configuration and maintenance of all Linux and Windows systems. The System Administrator will also support daily 24/7 operations, help develop strategies to improve availability and utilization of web application servers along with monitoring and alerting to provide high availability services to our clients. The ideal team member has strong experience with Windows/IIS systems and is comfortable with expanding his or her existing Linux skills. DevOps Certified administrator is comfortable with his or her existing scripting skills, as has experience with web services, configuration management using deployment automation tools, virtualization and other technologies throughout our course projects and assignments.

Participants will be able to work on windows 2008 plus IIS, linux/unix administrator, and performance tuning for IIS based web applications.A great understanding in virtualization technologies, VMware preferred In depth understanding of Linux/UNIX fundamentals including kernel subsystems, system calls, inter-process communication, process/resource management and file systems.

With Configuration management with deployment automations tools Monitoring and alerting, Demonstrated problem analysis and resolution skills Extensive knowledge of Apache, Tomcat, RHEL cluster including performance tuning for web applications, will be proficient in scripting language and Experience with load balancers.
