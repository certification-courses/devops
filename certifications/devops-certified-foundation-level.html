DevOps Certified Foundation Level

This foundation level of DevOps Certification gives an introduction to DevOps, a practice and professional movement that streses communication, collaboration, integration and automation in order to improve the flow of work between software developers and IT operation professional. Improved workflows will result in an improve ability to design, develop, deploy and operate software and services faster.

It will help you in understanding it's objectives and benefits to the business. The real concepts and practices which includes to Agile, Lean and IT Service Management.

This level have a different approach to understand improved workflows and Communication & Feedback loops.

After getting certified you will be able to apply DevOps in an enterprise environment, critical success factors and key performance indicators with real-life example and results.

This level is for Individuals and organizations seeking fundamental l understanding of DevOps. Employees, Managers and Suppliers who are contributing to an organisation's DevOps Initiatives. This will also helpful for consultants guiding their client through DevOps program and process improvement with application and infrastructure provides whose products are part of DevOps tool chain.
